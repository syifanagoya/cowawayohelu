Know All About Married At First Sight 2024 Cast and Release Date

August 25, 2024
blog

Cheers to everyone’s Married At First Sight season! Our favorite yearly show is in just a few weeks, and I can’t wait. Although there has been a lot of conjecture around the cast of MAFS 2024 since August of last year, our colleagues at Channel Nine have formally revealed the group of idealists taking a big chance on a nationwide reality program dedicated to love.

Matchmakers John Aiken, and Mel Schilling, and trained sexologist Alessandra Rampolla will pair ten couples in their search for love, and on their wedding day, they will first meet at the altar.

Let’s learn about cast members in the following paragraphs.

Andrea

Mafs 2024 cast Andrea is 51 years old, yet her youthful spirit shows that you don’t have to act your age or let a number define you. Andrea, a single mother of two adult children, left her most recent relationship after learning the term “gaslighting” from watching MAFS. She was motivated by the show to permanently sever her relationship with her ex and start a fresh journey in search of love.

She has experienced more bread-crumping, love-bombing, and ghosting since then than she can remember. Although Andrea thinks of herself as giving, kind, and kind, she also has a tattoo of a “To Do” list on her arm because she can occasionally be careless and accident-prone.

Cassandra

Mafs 2024 cast Cassandra is gregarious, vivacious, and a laughing sunshine. She claims to be able to communicate underwater, although she has experienced a lack of love. Following the heartbreaking demise of her initial romantic partner, Cassandra has encountered difficulties in establishing a significant relationship with anybody.

As her 30s draw near, she longs to find a partner who shares her values of love and family so they may start a life together and raise a “small football team” of children.

Eden

Mafs 2024 cast Eden has put a lot of effort into creating the life she loves, but because she lives on the Gold Coast and is separated from her family in Adelaide, she must rely on her best companion, her dog Cub, for company.

Since her spouse cheated on her with her closest friend, Eden’s previous relationship ended, and she has been single for three years. Eden acknowledges that her trust issues are deeply ingrained, having lost both her boyfriend and her longstanding closest friend in one fell swoop due to betrayal.

Ellie

Mafs 2024 cast Ellie, the eldest of three sisters, is eager to begin a family of her own and is very close to them. Ellie is sincere in her wish to find someone special to share her white picket fence ideas with, with the hopes of developing a stable relationship and becoming a mother.

After her ex-fiance deceived her a few years prior, Ellie was devastated and her wedding was called off one month before it was scheduled. She was completely shaken by the breakup of her romance and was left in continual need of comfort. She now acknowledges that she often goes above and above for individuals, even when she knows they don’t deserve it.

Lauren

Mafs 2024 cast Lauren always draws attention because of her incredibly sharp and sarcastic sense of humor. No matter the circumstance, she says it how she sees it and won’t put up with fools thanks to her striking appearance and strong convictions.

Lauren is a huge foodie who likes to cook and eat, especially with a robust glass of red wine. She runs a thriving hotel PR/marketing company, and her ideal date is dinner so she can judge her partner by the kind of restaurant he selects.

Referring to herself as “a little bit crazy” in romantic relationships, Lauren views conflict as a sign of passion and never backs down from a conversation. She seeks a steady, level-headed companion to counterbalance her. Despite her claim that she only attracts “red flags,” she hopes to meet a tall, dark, gorgeous man who will make her laugh till she tears up.

Lucinda

An “earth mama” with a firm sense of purpose, Lucinda loves to savor every moment of life, throwing lavish parties, taking many trips, and showing love to everyone she meets. By profession, Lucinda is a modern-day Cupid who owns a speed-dating business, performs as an MC and marriage celebrant, and hosts workshops and events to bring individuals from different backgrounds together. However, at 43, it’s her turn; she’s over heartbreak and is making room in her life for a companion.

Lucinda is seeking an accepting partner who isn’t afraid to show vulnerability, have fun, and see everything that the world has to offer. He needs to be committed to growing himself every day and realizing his greatest potential with her.

Natalie

Naturally a little bit nerdy, but proud of it, Natalie enjoys gaming, and cosplay, and has a sizable collection of mementos honoring her Samoyed dog, MieMie. The charming and sincere physiotherapist, who is 5’1″ (155 cm) tall with a personality larger than her stature, is searching for her own “Player 2” in life.

Natalie is a kind, loyal, and compassionate daughter who prioritizes her family and makes a special effort to keep in touch with those she cares about. Her fierce side can come out due to her loyalty, and she will defend herself and anyone else who is being treated unfairly.

Natalie enjoys being in love and is willing to put everything on the line. She is kind, supportive, and open-minded. 

Sara

Spanish-speaking Sara was born and reared in Colombia and migrated to Canada when she was a small child. She has spent the last eight years living in Australia. The light-haired “pretty boy” above “gym bro” is Sara’s ideal mate.

Sara is an honest communicator with a short fuse, but she worries that her blunt style may come across as harsh and that her candor may inadvertently offend someone. She attributes her feisty and outspoken personality to her Colombian ancestry.

Tori

According to Tori, who calls herself a “bulldog,” she doesn’t “do compromise.” Raised by powerful women, Tori is fiercely self-reliant. She doesn’t need a man, but if one were to come along, she would like to make room for him.

An alpha male who isn’t scared of a powerful woman would be Tori’s perfect match. He needs to be emotionally intelligent, dedicated, clean, physically healthy, focused on his work, and a skilled communicator. Tori acknowledges that her pickiness hasn’t helped her find the proper partner, despite having a long list of qualities she hopes her future partner must possess.

Ben

Ben has been a tour guide his entire life, so he’s used to walking away from relationships when things get tough, but he hasn’t found “the one” yet. 2019 saw the end of his previous committed relationship, and he finds dating draining.

When it comes to a companion, Ben is very particular. He detests “vanilla” and wants stimulation since he gets bored easily. He acknowledges that he occasionally has irrational expectations and a fear of settling, but he’s working to alter that.

Collins

Collins has absolutely no dating or relationship experience. He’s never had a girlfriend, has had one unrequited love break his heart, and is always in the friend zone.

Collins understands that he isn’t your normal tattooed gym junkie that easily draws girls, but he views himself as a sincere, polite, and courteous someone with lofty goals and a lot of love to share. When Collins meets his match on MAFS, he expects to finally break out from the friend zone and find that first spark.

Jack

Jack leads an extremely regimented and regulated life, and he has high aspirations and OCD together. His ideal spouse would have to meet a long list of requirements, including being attractive, athletic, smiling, tanned, and ideally brunette.

Jack is a born romantic, loves to place his women on a pedestal, and is extremely affectionate. He is hoping that the person he is partnered with will be patient enough to get to know him and show him unconditional affection for the real him.

Jayden

Jayden is accustomed to avoiding close relationships because he has a professional talent for masking his feelings and appearing painless.

Misinterpreted and frequently called a “bad F boy,” he is more complex than he appears. Women are drawn to Jayden in large quantities, but it’s never the appropriate type of attention. His ideal companion should be physically strong, self-sufficient, and ambitious, as well as willing to take on the task of dismantling his barriers.

Jonathan

After selling everything he owned and packing up his life, Jono spent 15 months traveling to over 50 countries. He is an adventurer and risk-taker at heart, having spent weeks at a time living on isolated islands and searching for his food to survive.

Jonathan believes he has been unfortunate in love and attributes his nearly 40-year-old single status to having dated wonderful women he should have wed in his 20s and making bad decisions in his 30s. It’s time for Jono to take his search for love seriously. He hopes to meet someone who is as driven and well-groomed as he is as he is excited to become a father.

Michael

Originating from the United States and raised in a small Ohioan town, Michael acknowledges that his upbringing was difficult at times, but he attributes his current self to his mother and strong family bonds.

Michael occasionally finds it difficult to down his guard in relationships because he worries about getting wounded. He is ready to settle down and is tired of the dating scene after five years of being single. He has big dreams of being a parent and starting a family of his own.

Michael is looking for a tall, dark, and attractive someone who shares his ideals and exudes confidence.

Richard

Richard, who considers himself to be youthful at heart, separated from his wife of 27 years, stating that the romance had faded. He recently ended a relationship with a woman who was 27 years his junior because she wanted children. Richard is unwilling to make any compromises because he already has three of his own.

Richard is looking for professionals to match him with a lady who possesses a flexible attitude, an enthusiasm for life, and an undeniable spark with him because he is finding it difficult to find a woman his age who meets his requirements.

Simon

Simon remains close to his former spouse and continues to share parental responsibilities for their teenage son. He’s been in a seven-year same-sex “situation” since they broke up, but it never really worked out, so he’s been looking for someone with whom to connect.

Recognizing his frequent feelings of loneliness, Simon says he is ready for love and longs for a serious relationship similar to his marriage, but this time with a guy. Since he doesn’t feel comfortable using dating apps, Simon hasn’t used any. He believes that he is a wonderful fit for MAFS.

Tim

Tim is a traditional businessman with ambitious financial objectives and a number of successful web ventures. He intends to construct a school in Ethiopia, the country from whence his sister was adopted, in the future.

After learning that his most recent girlfriend had cheated on him, Tim ended things with her. Dating is fun for him, but he keeps his barriers up because he hasn’t moved over his previous heartbreak. His ideal companion is open to the possibilities of the MAFS experience, has a strong sense of humour, and emotional stability, and emits feminine energy.

Timothy

Timothy recognized that he is lonely and longs for a partner’s affection, support, and emotional connection after recently losing his father. Though he has forgotten what it’s like to be in a relationship, he knows that he wants to experience butterflies and excitement as his bride comes down the aisle. His previous relationship ended six years ago.

Timothy does not have a specific physical type; instead, his ideal partner should be truly nice and have an easygoing, carefree attitude. He is prepared to look after someone and, in exchange, to have someone look after him. He would want to find love.

Tristan

Tristan describes his mother, who raised him and worked nonstop to support the family, as his hero, queen, and strongest ally.

He’s been hesitant to date again because his last relationship ended horribly, but he’s ready to give romance another go.

Having achieved success and passion in his job, Tristan is now prepared to shift his attention and become their go-to hype man. When it comes to his prospective spouse, he doesn’t have many requirements; all he wants is to be loved.
