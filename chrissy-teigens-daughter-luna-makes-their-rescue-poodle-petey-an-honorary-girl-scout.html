Chrissy Teigen and John Legend’s daughter, Luna, has been slanging Girl Scout Cookies and enlisted the help of their rescue poodle, Petey!

Luna made a promo video for #cookieseason.

The two adorable entrepreneurs both dressed up for the occasion! And Petey’s matching beret is peak cuteness. Petey was a rescue they adopted in January 2020, after Petey and his fur siblings were featured on Ellen Degeneres’ show. Kris Jenner also adopted one and named her Bridgette.

Teigen also posted an Instagram Story of Luna and Petey, where Luna offered a “free pet of the dog” with a cookie purchase. She misheard her daughter and questioned, “A free pet??” Luna confirmed she didn’t mean customers would get a complimentary dog, with Teigen replying, “Oh, you get to pet him. Okay, I thought you meant you were giving Petey away.” Luna then loudly stated, “NO!”
