What is Gabriel Rosado's Net Worth?

Gabriel Rosado is an American-Puerto Rican boxer who has a net worth of $300 thousand. Gabriel Rosado was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania in January 1986. His first professional boxing match came in January 2006 when he defeated Phil Hicklin. Rosado went 5-0 before losing his first match to Chris Gray in October 2006. In July 2010 he lost his first title fight to Derek Ennis for the USBA light middleweight title.

Rosado won his first title in September 2011 when he defeated Keenan Collins to win the vacant USA Pennsylvania state middleweight title. In June 2012 he beat Sechew Powell to win the vacant WBO Inter-Continental light middleweight title and in September 2012 he defeated Charles Whittaker to win the IBF Light Middleweight Title Eliminator. In January 2013 Rosado a match to Gennady Golovkin for the WBA World middleweight title and International Boxing Organization Middleweight title. He lost to Peter Quillin for the WBO middleweight title in October 2013 and lost his last fight against David Lemieux in December 2014 for the NABF Middleweight Title. As of December 2014 Rosado's record is 21 – 9 with 13 Knockouts.
